### Writing Style

**DO:**
- Use active voice: "We craft" not "Tables are crafted"
- Short sentences: 15-20 words maximum
- Concrete imagery: "oak grain" not "quality materials"
- First person: "We" not "The company"
- Present tense: "We create" not "We have created"

**DON'T:**
- Corporate jargon: "solutions," "leverage," "synergy"
- Superlatives: "best," "world-class," "unparalleled"
- Sales language: "buy now," "limited time," "exclusive offer"
- Passive voice: "Tables are made" not "We make tables"
- Technical specs (unless contextual): "MC 8-10%" â†’ "Carefully dried oak"
