# Project Brief: TWG Landing Page

## Executive Summary
TWG is launching a premium digital presence to elevate its reputation from a boutique workshop to a recognized partner for sustainable woodworking projects. The two-week initiative (November 11–22, 2025) focuses on pairing refined storytelling with a polished customer journey so prospective partners quickly understand the brand’s heritage, quality standards, and sustainability commitments. Success is defined by a timely launch, a memorable first impression, and a clear path from interest to qualified leads.

## Implementation Timeline

### Week 1: Foundation (November 11–15)
- Days 1–2: Align on final design direction, establish project infrastructure, and confirm content pathways that support the brand story.
- Days 3–4: Craft the landing experience with immersive hero storytelling, navigation flows, and initial product showcases that communicate credibility.
- Day 5: Complete detailed product narratives, refine visual interactions, and share a preview build for early validation.

### Week 2: Completion (November 18–22)
- Days 6–7: Deliver the manufacturing and sustainability narratives, integrate contact touchpoints, and begin systematic testing across core user journeys.
- Day 8: Optimize for performance, validate behavior across devices and browsers, and resolve prioritized issues.
- Day 9: Apply final polish, verify accessibility and content readiness, and prepare a client-facing preview.
- Day 10: Host the client review, apply final adjustments, and launch to production with a concise handoff package.

## Success Measures
- Achieve launch readiness by November 22, 2025 without schedule slippage.
- Deliver an experience that balances premium aesthetics with intuitive exploration of the brand’s craftsmanship and sustainability stories.
- Enable reliable lead capture with clear calls to action and supporting content.
- Maintain high engagement by encouraging visitors to explore multiple sections before exit.
